Elora Learning System

How Elora learns AI behaviour detection, governance adherence, runtime suitability, and emerging additional skills using non-neural, deterministic, and auditable methods.

Back to Research Open Documentation Model Wiki

Elora uses non-neural learning methods: Bayesian suitability scoring, case-based reasoning against prior runs, bucketed maturity tracking, and policy-bound routing decisions. This research first teaches Elora to detect AI behaviours for governance, then explores additional runtime skills not typically developed for inference control planes.

What Elora Learns

Model Suitability

Elora tracks which models perform best for specific task families using bucketed evidence, exam outcomes, and runtime stability signals.

Governance Adherence

Elora scores model adherence to governance controls across guardrail representations (code, markdown, sqlite3) and records confidence by sample depth and scenario coverage.

Observer Alignment

Observer outputs are scored for alignment with expected guarded outcomes, policy-control evidence, replay consistency, and behaviour-detection quality.

Operational Envelope

Elora learns practical stability bands from runtime behavior (latency, drift or collapse indicators, and safe context-pressure ranges) to support safer governance decisions.

How It Works

  • Measure: capture deterministic runtime and research outcomes.
  • Score: convert outcomes into explicit metrics (success, risk, adherence, confidence).
  • Store: update model/profile scoped learning state and evidence buckets.
  • Recommend: use learned evidence to suggest the best model profile per task family.
  • Govern: policy layer validates recommendations; the commit boundary remains authoritative.

Planned Expansion

Dynamic Observer Questions

Observer prompts will adapt by scenario family and policy-control focus so behaviour detection quality improves over repeated runs.

Governance Training Tracks

Dedicated scenario packs will compare guardrail methods and score control adherence consistency per model and profile.

Engine-Only Readiness

Readiness scoring will measure whether Elora can detect and classify breaches with reduced or no external guardrail scaffolding.

Additional Skill Research

Research expands into additional skills not typically associated with inference control planes, while retaining deterministic evidence, policy boundaries, and replay auditability.

What It Does Not Do

  • Does not train model weights.
  • Does not bypass policy or commit controls.
  • Does not use unconstrained or random routing in governed execution paths.

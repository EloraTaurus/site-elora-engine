# 01 Brand Foundations

## Positioning
Elora is a research-first, governance-first AI runtime system.

## Core Message
- Inference is proposal.
- Commit is authority.
- Decisions require evidence.

## Personality
- Technical
- Evidence-led
- Calm and precise
- Serious but readable

## Visual Direction
- White-first interface.
- Cool blue/violet accents.
- Dense information surfaces with clear structure.
- Minimal decorative noise in high-information sections.

## Non-Negotiables
- No critical decision text in low-contrast colors.
- No bright/neon accents that conflict with governance tone.
- No dark-mode-only assumptions in public pages.

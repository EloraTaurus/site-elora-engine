Our Use of Cookies

By default, analytics cookies are refused. You are opted out until you explicitly accept.

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics (GA4), a web analytics service provided by Google LLC ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies to help us understand website usage and engagement.

Information generated by these cookies (which can include your IP address and usage metadata) may be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google uses this information to evaluate website usage, compile reports, and provide related services.

Cookies used for Analytics (when you opt in)

  • _ga - used to distinguish users (up to 2 years).
  • _ga_<container-id> - used to persist session state (up to 2 years).
  • _gcl_au - may be set by Google services for conversion/measurement.

Your choice

You can accept or deny analytics cookies at any time using the cookie icon at the bottom-left of the site. Your preference is stored locally in your browser and can be changed whenever you want.
